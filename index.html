DeCaffeine

DeCaffeine is a French startup created in 2023. It allows coffee lovers to consume coffee at any time of the day thanks to its innovative formula. Indeed, the coffee served in each DeCaffeine lounge can be caffeine-free (at the customer's request) without changing the taste.
